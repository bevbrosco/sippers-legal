Account

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, tap Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Account deletion is permanent and removes your profile, all logged Bevs and Sips, follows, and uploaded photos. This action cannot be undone.

How do I change my username or display name?

Tap Profile → Edit Profile. You can change your display name at any time. Usernames can be changed once every 14 days.

How do I make my profile private?

Tap Profile → Settings → Privacy and toggle Private Account. New followers will need to be approved before they can see your reviews.

Content + Safety

How do I report a review or user?

On any review, tap the … menu and choose Report. To report a user, open their profile and tap … → Report. We review all reports and typically respond within 48 hours.

How do I block someone?

From the user's profile, tap … → Block. They will no longer be able to see your reviews, follow you, or appear in your feed.

What's the minimum age?

Sippers is for users 17 and older. Age is verified during onboarding via a date-of-birth gate.

Data + Privacy

How is my data protected?

See our Privacy Policy for the full breakdown. Short version: we don't sell your data, we use industry-standard encryption, and your reviews are visible according to your privacy settings.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Email bevbrosco@gmail.com from the address tied to your account and we'll send you a copy of your reviews, sips, and profile data.
